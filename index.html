Who are we?

Hello everyone. We are Rikke & Casper Holt Farver. Together we have a passion for photography. HF Photography is all about this passion and sharing it all with you. We love the nature. We love people, their actions, and sincere emotions. We try to capture all of this and much more from many different ankles & directions. If you are interested to see The World From Our Perspective, feel free to come along on this wonderful journey.
